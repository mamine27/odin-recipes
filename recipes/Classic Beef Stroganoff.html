Classic Beef Stroganoff Recipe

Description

Classic Beef Stroganoff is a hearty and comforting dish made with tender strips of beef cooked in a creamy mushroom sauce, served over egg noodles. This dish is known for its rich and savory flavors, making it a favorite for family dinners.

Main Ingredients

  1. 1 lb (450g) beef sirloin or tenderloin, thinly sliced
  2. Salt and pepper to taste
  3. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  4. 1 onion, finely chopped
  5. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  6. 8 oz (225g) mushrooms, sliced
  7. 1 cup beef broth
  8. 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  9. 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  10. 1 cup sour cream
  11. 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  12. 8 oz (225g) egg noodles
  13. Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps to Make Classic Beef Stroganoff

  1. Season the beef strips with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the beef strips and cook until browned. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they are tender and browned.
  5. Stir in the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and Dijon mustard. Bring to a simmer.
  6. In a small bowl, mix the sour cream and flour until smooth. Gradually add this mixture to the skillet, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.
  7. Return the cooked beef strips to the skillet and simmer for an additional 5 minutes, or until the beef is heated through.
  8. Meanwhile, cook the egg noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  9. Serve the beef stroganoff over the cooked egg noodles and garnish with chopped parsley.
