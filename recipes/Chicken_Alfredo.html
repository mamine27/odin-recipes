Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy and delicious pasta dish made with tender pieces of chicken, a rich Alfredo sauce, and fettuccine noodles. This dish is a favorite for its comforting flavors and satisfying texture.

Main Ingredients

  1. 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  2. Salt and pepper to taste
  3. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  4. 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  5. 1/2 cup butter
  6. 2 cups heavy cream
  7. 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  8. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  9. 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  10. 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
  11. 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  12. Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps to Make Chicken Alfredo

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to low and simmer for about 5 minutes, or until the sauce begins to thicken.
  6. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and Italian seasoning to the sauce, stirring until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth. If using, add a pinch of nutmeg.
  7. Slice the cooked chicken breasts into thin strips.
  8. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta and sliced chicken to the skillet, tossing to coat in the Alfredo sauce.
  9. Sprinkle the grated mozzarella cheese over the top and continue to cook until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
